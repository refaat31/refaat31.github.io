I was looking for Arabic series on Netflix recently. Then I came across this series called - Tahir's House. I think my fellow third culture kids will enjoy this one. This is a feel-good and comedy series. 


## What is it about

Yousuf is a married man that is having troubles at home - his wife essentially kicked him out for as long as he doesn't land a job and his father doesn't want him to
live in their home. Yousef is portrayed as a lazy person.


### A trip down memory lane

The streets shown in the series reminded me of the home I have known for an entire lifetime. The home I cannot return to. I miss having *fava beans* (also called Ful in Arabic) with *Tamees*.
